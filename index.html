Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 24, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Moodle Task Viewer for Ruppin" Chrome Extension (the "Extension") handles user information.

Core Privacy Promise: We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. All processing happens locally on your machine.

1. Information Collection and Use

The Extension interacts with the Ruppin Academic Center Moodle website to display your academic tasks.

  • Personal Data: The Extension does NOT collect personal information such as names, emails, or login credentials.
  • Academic Data: The Extension reads task names, deadlines, and course titles from your Moodle dashboard. This data is used solely to provide the core functionality of the extension (viewing tasks).
  • Usage Data: We do not track how you use the extension or what websites you visit.

2. Data Storage and Security

All data retrieved from Moodle is stored locally within your browser's storage. No data is ever uploaded to a server, cloud service, or shared with third parties. When you remove the extension, all locally stored data is deleted.

3. Limited Use Disclosure

The Extension complies with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We only use Moodle data to provide a task-viewing interface and do not use it for any other purpose.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or trade your data with any third parties. There are no advertisements or analytics trackers embedded in the extension.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can open an issue on our GitHub repository: Hydrolicks/Moodle-Task-Viewer-For-Ruppin.
